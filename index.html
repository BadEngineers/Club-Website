There are two ways of constructing a software design. One way is to make it so simple that there are obviously no deficiencies. And the other way is to make it so complicated that there are no obvious deficiencies. Tony Hoare

The CPC aims to bring together SSU students interested in computer programming and other related topics, to learn and share within a community of like-minded individuals.

The club also raises awareness of relevant computer science and software engineering issues and interesting topics within the field. We also host competitions, technical workshops, and other actives.


Interested in joining the club? Just come to one of our meetings!

Meetings

Weekly meetings where we discuss intersting topics, and practice programming problems.

Contests

Yearly programming contests where you can prove your skills and compete for prizes.

Workshops

Student and faculty-led workshops in a different number of topics.

Gaming Events

From League of Legends to Mario Kart and Smash tournaments. If you like gaming, join us for some competitive fun!

We always try to provide food at all our events and meetings!
