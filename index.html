The question of whether a computer can think is no more interesting than the question of whether a submarine can swim. Edsger W. Dijkstra

The Computer Science club brings SSU students interested in computer computer science, and other related topics, together within the SSU community.

CSC also raises awareness of computer science issues, interesting topics within the field, hosts competitions and workshops, etc.

Meetings

Weekly meetings where we discuss intersting topics, and practice coding challenges from websites like HackerRank and LeetCode.

Contests

Yearly programming contests where you can prove your skills and compete for prizes.

Workshops

Student or Faculty-led workshops in a different number of topics, so you can keep your skills up to date.

Gaming Events

From LAN parties to Mario Kart and Smash tournaments. If you like gaming, we like you!

Plus, we always try to provide food at all our events and meetings!
