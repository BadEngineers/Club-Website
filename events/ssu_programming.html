2nd SSU Programming Contest

The 2nd SSU Programming Contest was held on February 27, 2013, Weddnesday, from 4:30pm~7:30pm. It was sponsored by the Computer Science department and Programming Club. Dr. Beifang Yi was the organizer of the contest.
